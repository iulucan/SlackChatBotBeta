# GreenLeaf Logistics Internal Handbook
**Version:** 2.1 (Sanitized for AI Assistant)
**Classification:** INTERNAL USE ONLY

---

## 1. Company History
Founded in 1974 by Hans Egli in Basel, GreenLeaf Logistics began with a single truck and a commitment to Swiss precision. By 1985, the fleet grew to ten trucks, and in 1998, the company pioneered digital warehouse management in the region. Our headquarters remains in Basel, operating under the motto: "A clean truck is a happy truck."

## 2. Office Etiquette & Communal Spaces
* **Kitchen:** Shared fridge items must be labeled with [NAME] and [DATE]. Unlabeled items are discarded every Friday at 16:00.
* **Microwave:** Users must use covers and are responsible for immediate cleaning after use.
* **Office Plants:** Maintenance is handled exclusively by professional contractors ("GreenServices") on the first Tuesday of every month. Do not water the plants.

## 3. Working Hours & Attendance
GreenLeaf operates on a trust-based flextime model.
* **Core Hours:** 08:30 to 17:30.
* **Lunch Break:** Mandatory 45-minute break.
* **Customer Support:** Operates on a shift rotation (refer to official IT schedules).
* **Warehouse Staff (Basel-Stadt):** Must be onsite by 07:00 due to loading bay regulations.

## 4. Time Off (Vacation & Holidays)
* **Annual Leave:** 25 days of paid annual leave per calendar year for full-time employees.
* **Seniority Bonus:** Employees over the age of 50 receive an additional 5 days (Total: 30 days).
* **Public Holidays:** We observe all national Swiss holidays.
* **Basel-Stadt Specific:** Labor Day (May 1st) is observed as a **full holiday** for all staff based in the Basel-Stadt Canton.
* **Request Process:** Requests must be submitted via the HR portal at least 3 weeks in advance. Final approval is managed by the Head of Operations.

## 5. Bereavement & Special Leave
* **Immediate Family:** 3 days of paid leave (spouse, child, or parent).
* **Close Relatives:** 1 day of paid leave (grandparents or siblings).
* **Extended Requests:** Any request exceeding 3 days requires a personal meeting and written approval from the CEO.

## 6. IT & Security
* **Passwords:** Must be changed every 90 days. Sharing or writing down passwords is strictly prohibited.
* **Network Access:** * **Internal:** Device registration is required via IT Department.
    * **Guest:** Temporary access is available for visitors. Password rotates annually (Contact IT for current credentials).
* **Hardware:** Laptops are company property. In case of damage (e.g., liquid spills), bring the device immediately to the IT desk. Do not attempt manual repairs.

## 7. Expenses & Travel
* **Client Lunches:** Only reimbursable if at least one external client is present.
* **Limits:** Maximum **35 CHF per person**.
* **Alcohol Policy:** Strictly **non-reimbursable**. Alcohol must be paid for on a separate personal receipt and will not be processed for company repayment.
* **Submission:** All receipts must be scanned using the "ScanPro" app. Physical paper or photos of crumpled receipts are not accepted.

## 8. Safety & Emergency Procedures
* **Fire Alarms:** Exit via the nearest stairwell. Do not use elevators.
* **Assembly Point:** The gravel parking lot behind the main warehouse.
* **Fire Wardens:** Each floor has a designated warden (refer to posted signage in the building).

## 9. Conduct & Conflict Resolution
* **Conflict:** Employees should first attempt a "Coffee Chat" to resolve peer disputes.
* **Serious Misconduct:** Matters regarding harassment, bullying, or whistleblowing must be handled through the external Ombudsman to ensure anonymity.
* **Confidentiality:** Do not discuss sensitive misconduct or legal matters with the internal AI bot or via public Slack channels.
* **Ombudsman Contact:** ombudsman@greenleaf-safety.ch
